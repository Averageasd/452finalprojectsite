About

This is a very ambitious project that combines RTS concepts with VR. As a main player in this game, we are in charge of small-sized army that contains 20 ranged soldiers. Our main job, as a player, is very simple: Defend the castles and defeat all enemies. The game is simple to learn, but hard to master. We can determine the outcome of the game. To secure final victory, we have to master the art of using the allied solders. These soldiers can be easily killed by enemies if each of them is isolated from the group. Moreover, their damage and health are low. It is very important to keep track of our soldiers and constantly put them in safe spots instead of letting them to get to close to the enemies, who are agile, highly durable and have an ability to do AOE damage.
